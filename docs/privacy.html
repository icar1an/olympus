Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

Mode ("we", "our", or "the extension") that extracts aesthetics from your Pinterest boards or any image, and surfaces historical runway shows that capture a feeling you can curate but can't fully describe. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our extension.

Information We Collect

Images You Analyze

When you use Mode to analyze an image, the image data is sent to our servers for processing. We use this data solely to provide you with analysis results.

  • Images are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers
  • Analysis results may be cached temporarily to improve performance
  • If you save an analysis to your collection, the results (not the original image) are stored in your account

Account Information

To prevent spam and fake users, account sign-in with Google is required. When you sign in, we collect:

  • Email address — to identify your account
  • Display name — to personalize your experience
  • Profile photo — to display in the extension interface

Account sign-in is mandatory to prevent spam and ensure the integrity of our platform.

Pinterest Integration

If you connect your Pinterest account, we access:

  • Your public boards and pins (read-only)
  • Images from pins you choose to analyze

We do not post to your Pinterest account or access private boards without your explicit selection.

Local Storage

The extension stores data locally on your device:

  • Recent analysis history
  • Your preferences and settings
  • Cached data for offline access

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly sync it by signing in.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide analysis of images you submit
  • Generate references and context
  • Create shareable cards
  • Sync your saved collections across devices (if signed in)
  • Improve and optimize the extension's performance

Third-Party Services

Mode uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — for authentication, database, and cloud functions
  • Pinterest API — for board integration (if connected)

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Pinterest Privacy Policy

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Authentication tokens are securely stored
  • We use Firebase's security infrastructure
  • We do not store raw images on our servers

Data Retention

  • Analyzed images: Processed in real-time, not retained
  • Analysis results: Retained while your account is active
  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Local data: Retained until you clear extension data or uninstall

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data through the extension interface
  • Delete your saved analyses and collections
  • Disconnect third-party integrations (Pinterest)
  • Delete your account and all associated data

Children's Privacy

Mode is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at mode@yulong.co.
