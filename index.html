How it works

Three ways to capture aesthetics

Click any image

Browse the web normally. When you see an image that inspires you, simply click to capture and analyze its aesthetic.

Upload from device

Have a saved image or screenshot? Upload directly from your computer for instant aesthetic analysis.

Paste a URL

Found an image link? Paste it directly into Prometheus for quick analysis without leaving your workflow.

Deep aesthetic analysis

Powered by advanced AI

Aesthetic Identification

Identifies the core aesthetic — from dark academia to avant-garde, minimalist to maximalist, and everything in between.

Runway References

Connects your image to relevant designer collections and runway shows throughout fashion history.

Detailed Breakdown

Explores silhouette, texture, color palette, and styling details that define the look.

Shareable Cards

Generate beautiful, shareable cards to document and share your aesthetic discoveries.

Pinterest Integration

Connect your Pinterest account to analyze entire boards at once. Perfect for decoding the aesthetic thread running through your saved inspiration.
